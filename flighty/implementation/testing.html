Code as resilient as a Willow bouncer. Structured to hold its shit together so you don't have to.

It's 5 in the morning, Laurence has put on some full-volume German techno, and you feel ready to projectile vomit across a row of monitors. But Flighty is so well made that you hold it together and enter a crazed-hyper-productive beast-mode. It's just that good.

G-Unit. Unit testing implemented out of the box.

Flighty was tested extensively throughout its development cycle, and many tests implemented. These tests have been included in the source for you to take forward saving you valuable time, without sacrificing reliability for the end-user.
